Why should I join Hackers@UPC?

Joining Hackers@UPC will bring you a lot of new challenges and experience. You'll be able to meet and work with an awesome team full of passionate people, learn a lot of new skills and help grow a community by organizing amazing events which bring people together! Being part of an association like Hackers@UPC can let experience college life in a different and fun way.

❮ ❯

What does it mean to be part of Hackers@UPC?

Being part of Hackers@UPC means work and cooperate with other people, have meetings regularly, be constant, and perform well under pressure. Without passion and commitment from the organizers, HackUPC can't thrive! In the end, being able to meet the hackers and experience a great weekend with everyone shows how hard work pays off!

Our Departments

In HackUPC, we are structured by departments. Each department has a specific role in the development of the event, and being part of a department doesn't mean that you need to be only involved in that department.

Sponsorship

Like any event, HackUPC has a financial support which is provided by the amazing sponsors that attend our event and present challenges for the hackers, and used to provide you with cool stuff during the event, like food, swag, etc. And getting that budget is where the Sponsorship team comes into play! To be part of this team, you need to be willing to practise your english and be ready to have meetings with sponsors and negotiate with them.

TheGame

HackUPC holds a 24h CTF during the event. We need your skills to bring this unique and awesome experience to the hackers.

Marketing

HackUPC wouldn't be possible without the hackers, so our job is to promote all of our association's events to everyone! We take charge of maintaining our social media active, creating content regularly, and answering all your questions or doubts. We need creative minds and people who are up to date with social media!

Logistics

During HackUPC, we deal with lots of logistic challenges: providing the hackers with food (a lot) and swag, a place to work or to sleep in, setting up the cafeteria (so they can continue eating), looking for snack partners, and much more. That's where our department comes in! We are in charge of managing all of those challenges and more. If you are active, comfortable with teamwork, and want to help us, the Logistics team is right for you!

HackerXperience

We want the hackers to have the best experience possible at HackUPC! from the beginning until the end of the event. We design fun activities, ensure fair judging criteria for the projects, look for mentors to helps the hackers, and much more! If you are creative and want to enhance the hacker's experience, join our team!

Legal & Finance

Hackers@UPC is a very active association and we organize events of different magnitudes, so a financial gestion that meets expectations is fundamental to us. Knowing how the association's budget is spent is crucial to make everything work, and that's the task of this department! We manage the budget and protect the association legally, so being curious and eager to learn new stuff is the basic requirement to be part of the Legal & Finance team!

Staff

During HackUPC, we need extra hands to set up the venue and leave everything ready for the hackers, so our team's task is to recruit the amazing volunteers who come and help us do this. We coordinate and organize the volunteers to work efficiently, so if you are an organized person who can be a leader, this might be the place for you!

Design

For HackUPC, the design is really important because it is the way we have to communicate our work. It makes it possible to connect our events with our hackers, sponsors, and partners. Our job is to design everything related to the events of the association, including the most important one, HackUPC. Websites, our social media image, stickers, t-shirts, event decoration, and much more is what we do at our team!

WebDev

HackUPC wouldn't be possible without our web pages and services. At WebDev, we develop and implement these websites to simplify work for hackers and organizers, and make the hackathon smoother. We use modern technologies such as Django, HTML & JS, Vue.js, as well as others. We are looking for people with basic programming knowledge and, above all, a strong will to learn new languages and technology.
